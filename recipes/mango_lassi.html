Mango Lassi

Description

Mango lassi is a popular Indian drink consumed largely in the summer.

It only contains a few ingredients and is easy to put together for a refreshing beverage to enjoy in the heat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mango
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup ice

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend for 2 minutes.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
