Oreo Milkshake

Description

Milk's best friend, Oreo, paired together in this classic milkshake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 8 Oreo cookies
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. Add ice cream, milk, and Oreo cookies to a blender.
  2. Blend until mostly smooth.
  3. Serve with whipped cream and chocolate syrup if desired and enjoy!
